ABOUT KBL

KBL is N/NPT and MO Certified Company by American Society of Mechanical Engineers for to manufacture Nuclear Quality Class 1 Pump & components.

Manufacturing, fabrication and testing of Secondary Sodium Pump is very critical and to manufacture these unique pumps, special facilities are established at KBL’s Kirloskarwadi manufacturing plant like new test set-up, dedicated SS fabrication shop at vendor’s place, vertical heat treatment furnace for long shaft of about 6 metres length, dynamic balancing machine for long rotor, various critical machines for manufacturing components, Nuclear clean hall for assembly of pump.

We at Kirloskar Brothers Limited are really proud to be the ONLY pump manufacturing company in INDIA to develop Sodium pumps (Primary Heat Transport Pumps) for Prototype Fast Breeder Reactor.

ABOUT SSP

Secondary Sodium Pumps are in the Secondary circuit and main objective of SSP is to circulate the liquid sodium through the secondary sodium circuit to extract heat from the Intermediate heat exchanger and transfer it to the steam water circuit through steam generators. SSP also removes the decay heat, extracted by the primary coolant from the core during reactor shut down.

The Secondary Sodium Pump is centrifugal; bottom suction and side double discharge, type Pump. The space above sodium free surface is filled with an argon cover gas with pressure of 0.4 MPa (abs). The pump is of vertical pull out construction type.

SSP delivers 3340 m3/sec flows and it develops the head of 65 metres at nominal speed of 900 rpm. The efficiency of Pump is 83 %.

Liquid handled by this pump is Sodium with a temperature from 350˚C to 550˚C. Total weigh of the pump is 25 tonnes. The pump is driven by 2700 kW induction motor having weight of 26 tonnes.

The material of construction of SSP is basically Stainless Steel grade 304LN forgings and CF3 castings. Components manufactured are nuclear quality level class 1, and the estimated life of Secondary sodium pump is 40 years.

SSP has one suction nozzle and two delivery nozzles. Suction nozzle has a diameter of 800 mm; while both the delivery nozzles have a diameter of 600 mm.

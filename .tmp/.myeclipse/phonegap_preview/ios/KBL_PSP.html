ABOUT KBL

Kirloskar Brothers Limited is proud to be associated with Department of Atomic Energy who are setting up the development of 500 MWe. Prototype Fast Breeder Reactor (PFBR) at BHARTIYA NABHIKIYA VIDYUT NIGAM LIMITED in association with INDIRA GANDHI CENTRE FOR ATOMIC RESEARCH (IGCAR), Kalpakkam.

KBL is N/NPT and MO Certified Company by American Society of Mechanical Engineers for to manufacture Nuclear Quality Class 1 Pump & components.

Primary Sodium Pump is the only rotating element in the reactor. Hence, this pump is called as the heart of the reactor. To manufacture these unique pumps, special facilities are established at KBL’s Kirloskarwadi manufacturing plant like new test set-up, vertical heat treatment furnace for long shaft of about 12 metres length, dynamic balancing machine for 15 metres long rotor, various critical machines for manufacturing components, Nuclear clean hall for assembly of pump.

We at Kirloskar Brothers Limited are really proud to be the ONLY pump manufacturing company in INDIA to develop Sodium pumps (Primary Heat Transport Pumps) for Prototype Fast Breeder Reactor.

ABOUT PSP

Primary Sodium Pump is installed in reactor and main objective of PSP is to transfer heat from Nuclear reaction to the secondary circuit by circulating the high temperature liquid Sodium.

The Primary Sodium Pump is a vertical, single stage, top suction, free surface pump. The pump is having Sodium lubricated hydrostatic bottom bearing whereas top bearing is of oil bath lubricated tilting pad type. The pump impeller takes suction from the sodium pool at 4000 C in the main vessel and discharges into an axial diffuser and delivery pipe.

PSP delivers 4300 litres of Sodium per second and develops a head of 75 metres of liquid column at nominal speed of 590 rpm. The efficiency of pump is 82 %.

Liquid handled by this pump is sodium with a temperature ranging from 400˚C to 550˚C. Total weight of the pump is 45 tonnes and length is 15 meters. The pump is driven by 3600 kW Induction motor which has flywheel at the top. The total weight of motor is 45 tonnes.

The material of construction of PSP largely is Stainless Steel grade 304LN forgings and CF3 castings. Components manufactured are nuclear quality level class 1, and the estimated life of primary sodium pump is 40 years.
